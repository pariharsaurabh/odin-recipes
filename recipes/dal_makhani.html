DAL MAKHANI

Description

Dal Makhani is an indian dish which is super tasty and delicious. It's protein rich and is generally eaten with tandoori roti or a Naan.

It's generally made in north india and is very common in regular house holds.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Onion
  • Vigna Mungo
  • Garam Masala
  • Chilli pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the Vigna mungo in water for about 45 mins.
  2. Fry onion and garam masala in butter.
  3. Now, add chilli pepper to the frying pan.
  4. Once the gravy is ready add Vigna Mungo to the gravy.
  5. The dish is ready to eat and serve.
